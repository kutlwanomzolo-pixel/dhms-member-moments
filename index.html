02 / Cultural diagnosis

The Insights

Human insight

Healthcare is rarely experienced individually.

It is experienced collectively: as caregiving, coordination, pressure, support and survival planning.

Cultural insight

South Africans process stress through humour, storytelling and shared language.

The category has completely underutilised recognisable South African behaviour.

Internet insight

Comment sections are now more culturally important than posts themselves.

People add stories, remix experiences, compare family behaviour and emotionally participate.

Why now / 2026

Recognition is the new virality.

The creator economy has shifted from “look at my life” to “this happened to me too.”

05 / Content ecosystem

TikTok

Primary platform. Instagram

Reels for emotional amplification. Stories for polls, submissions, anonymous confessions and engagement mining.

YouTube

Long-form storytelling can extend Member Moments into richer, more immersive narratives as an optional add-on within the Option 3 pricing structure.

09 / Rollout

From seeding to participation

A 6 week launch architecture that starts with familiar behaviour and ends with a platform the community can claim.

Week 1–2

Seed culture

Launch recognisable moments, humour territories and conversational storytelling. Goal: trigger recognition.

Week 2–4

Amplification

Release the second creator post as a natural extension of the first Member Moment. It can deepen the story, answer a comment from launch or show the “what happened next” moment. Goal: reinforce recognition.

Week 4–6

Community participation

Mine comments, story replies and audience submissions. Use Stories for polls, prompts, confessions and “tell us yours” mechanics that turn viewers into contributors. Goal: participation.

Week 6+

Member Moments Mashup Series

A standalone podcast video series that uses Member Moments as a conversation starter for bigger discussions around health, family, lifestyle and the value of being covered. The format could move between intimate interviews, creator hosted panels, member stories and expert led education, giving DHMS multiple storytelling moments beyond the campaign. The goal would be to build a repeatable content property.

11 / Reporting + KPIs

KPI driven reporting

Tru Blaq will provide a comprehensive campaign report that connects exposure, meaningful participation, cost efficiency and audience perception to clear learnings and recommendations.

01 / Campaign impact

Impressions + reach

Tracks campaign visibility and awareness. It is not the primary success measure, but shows whether Member Moments reached enough people to build recognition and momentum.

02 / Primary focus

Meaningful engagement

Likes will be tracked, but priority will be given to comments, shares, saves, tags, stitches and replies. This shows whether the content is prompting real recognition and conversation.

CommentsSharesSavesTagsStitches
03 / Value for money

Cost per engagement

Shows the cost of each meaningful interaction. We will compare creators, platforms and content territories to see where the budget worked hardest.

04 / Perception shift

Sentiment + scoring

Tracks whether conversation is positive, neutral or negative, and what themes are driving it. This helps show if Member Moments is shifting perception in a more human direction.

Final deliverable

Data translated into direction.

Reporting will be supported by Klear by Meltwater, giving DHMS a credible view of creator performance, audience quality, campaign delivery and content impact. The final report will translate the data into clear learnings, creator insights and practical recommendations for what to retain, refine and scale next.
